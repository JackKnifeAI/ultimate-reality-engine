## SAFETY CONSIDERATIONS

### Vacuum Safety
- **Implosion risk**: Acrylic chambers can implode; use safety shields
- **High voltage**: Piezo controllers operate at 150V; use proper insulation
- **Laser safety**: Class 3B lasers require safety goggles and interlocks

### Electrical Safety
- **Grounding**: All metal parts must be properly grounded
- **Interlocks**: Chamber door must disable laser and HV when open
- **Emergency stop**: Kill all power with single button

### Material Safety
- **Solvents**: Use acetone and IPA in ventilated area
- **Gold sputtering**: Requires vacuum deposition chamber and training
- **Cryogenics**: Liquid nitrogen requires proper handling (gloves, face shield)
